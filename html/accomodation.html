Okaeri Hotel Rooms & Pavilions

Whether you're here for business or leisure, we aim to make your stay as comfortable and enjoyable as possible.

All stays include

  • Access to hot-spring onsen bathing facilities fed by mineral-rich waters
  • Evening aperitif with complimentary beer, wine, champagne, and more, accompanied by small snacks
  • Daily Japanese style afternoon tea ”Kanmi” with Japanese tea selection including Matcha green tea
  • Complimentary soft drink selection available at The Living Pavilion throughout the day
  • Daily breakfast at The Living Pavilion, where an extensive menu of Western and Japanese dishes offers a nourishing and unlimited feast.
  • Guided tour of Okaeri Kyoto's secret garden
