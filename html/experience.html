Wellness

Wellness is seamlessly integrated into every aspect of the guest experience. Wellness in a Japandi hotel goes beyond just physical health; it encompasses mental tranquility and emotional balance.

Guests can indulge in a variety of wellness activities, such as yoga and meditation classes held in serene garden spaces or traditional tatami rooms. There might also be opportunities for forest bathing or nature walks in nearby green spaces.

Okaeri hotel is about creating a holistic experience that nourishes the body, mind, and soul, leaving guests feeling refreshed, rejuvenated, and deeply connected to themselves and the world around them.

Dinning

Guests can expect an exquisite culinary experience that showcases the artistry and precision of Japanese cuisine. The chef's expertise lies not only in the technical skills required to prepare traditional Japanese dishes but also in the ability to infuse them with creativity and innovation.

The menu may feature a variety of authentic Japanese dishes, from delicate sushi and sashimi to hearty ramen and comforting donburi bowls. Each dish is crafted with the finest ingredients, with an emphasis on seasonality and freshness.

Guests can also look forward to experiencing the omakase dining style, where the chef curates a multi-course meal based on the freshest ingredients available that day.

Celebration

The resort caters to weddings and celebrations both small and grand, with the opportunity to make Okaeri home through a private buyout of the entire resort, allowing you and your guests free reign of the secret garden surroundings.

Celebrations can further make use of the resort’s extensive facilities, including hot springs for onsen bathing, and exceptional dining – from picnics among the flora to cocktail parties on the expansive garden terrace.

At the end of the evening, as the newlyweds bid farewell to their guests, they can take comfort in knowing that their wedding celebration in a Japandi hotel was a truly magical and unforgettable experience, filled with love, laughter, and the timeless beauty of two rich cultural traditions coming together as one.
